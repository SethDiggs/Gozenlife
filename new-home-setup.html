White Glove Tech Relocation & New Home or Business Setup We Plug, You Play Zen Life Handles Your Tech

At Zen Life, we understand how stressful moving days can be for your home or business. Our vision is to transform the chaos of moving into a seamless, soulful experience,where technology meets concierge-level service to support your life from day one.

Zen Life offers a complete, turnkey solution for relocating and setting up your technologyexpertly, elegantly, and energetically. From service transfers and site preparation to disassembly, transport, and reinstallation, we manage your entire tech ecosystem with luxury-level attention to detail.

We even coordinate with your movers and service providers so everything works perfectly the same day you move in.

And yes, we bring a big bag of organic popcornbecause your first night should be Netflix-ready, not nerve-wracking.

Our Signature Services Include:


  • Expert Consultation: We start with a walk-through of your current systems and new spaces, helping us create a custom plan tailored to your lifestyle, tech requirements and emotional needs. With a clear plan and communication you'll know what to expect every step of the way, and well ensure minimal downtime throughout the process.

  • Site Preparation: Your systems are disassembled, transported, and reassembled by technicians who know exactly how to handle them.

  • White Glove Tech Disassembly & Packing: Have your movers take it or ask us help, we expertly dismantle and pack everything using shock-resistant materials and meticulous labeling systems. Each item is cleaned, protected, and prepared for flawless reassembly.

  • Coordinated Transport: We work directly with your movers to schedule the arrival and reinstallation of your technology.

  • Same-Day Seamless Reinstallation: The same day you move in is the same day you can sit on your couch, remote in hand, with your WiFi and TV working with a complimentary selection of organic comfort foods so you dont have to go digging through brown boxes to find a snack.

  • Service Transfer Made Simple: Avoid painful hours of hold music and frustrating tech service calls. We handle all provider communications and transfers for you, from AT&T to Xfinity. We update your service address, coordinate installations, and ensure everything is online the moment you arrive.
